## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Start Small**: Don't try to customize everything at once. Start with essential sections.
2. **Keep It Simple**: Less is more. Remove sections you don't need.
3. **Use Real Content**: Avoid lorem ipsum. Use real testimonials and actual copy.
4. **Mobile First**: Always check mobile view as you customize.
5. **Consistent Branding**: Use the same colors, fonts, and tone throughout.
6. **Clear CTAs**: Make your calls-to-action obvious and action-oriented.
7. **Fast Loading**: Compress images before uploading.
8. **Test Everything**: Click every link and button before launching.
