Common Questions

Everything you need to know about at-home lab testing in Bali.

Still have questions? Contact us
How does delivery work in Bali?

Our trained staff delivers test kits directly to your villa, apartment, hotel, or coworking space across Canggu, Seminyak, Ubud, Sanur, and surrounding areas. We coordinate delivery times via WhatsApp to ensure you're home. Delivery is fast, discreet, and included in all plans.

How long do results take?

Most results are available within 24-48 hours for standard tests (STD, urine/feces scan). More comprehensive tests like Bali Belly or specialized panels may take 2-4 days. You'll receive a push notification in the app as soon as your results are ready.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. All health data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared with third parties. We comply with international privacy standards. Your test results are only visible to you in your private app account. Discreet packaging and confidential handling are guaranteed.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your monthly plan anytime through the app or by contacting us. If you cancel mid-month, you'll still receive that month's kit, but won't be charged again.

Do you provide medical treatment or prescriptions?

No. balitestkits provides testing and wellness monitoring for informational purposes. We don't diagnose, treat, or prescribe medications. If your results indicate you need care, we'll provide clear guidance and can connect you with trusted local clinics or doctors in Bali.

What areas of Bali do you serve?

We currently deliver to Canggu, Seminyak, Kerobokan, Ubud, Sanur, Denpasar, and surrounding areas. Coverage is expanding! If you're outside these zones, join the waitlist and let us know your location, we're adding new areas based on demand.

What if I'm only in Bali for a few months?

Perfect! You can subscribe for just the months you're here or book one-time tests as needed. No commitment required. Many digital nomads use our service during their Bali stays to stay on top of their health without the clinic hassle.
